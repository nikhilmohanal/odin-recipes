Mojito Recipe

A yummy twist on the classic mojito.

Ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces silver tequila (such as Patron®)
  • 12 leaves mint
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 3 lime wedges
  • ¼ cup club soda, or as needed

    • Steps

    1. Combine tequila, mint leaves, sugar, and lime wedges in a tall glass; vigorously stir the mixture, crushing mint with the back of a spoon to release oils and squeezing juice from the lemon wedges. Top with club soda and stir gently to serve.
